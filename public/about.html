About LocalMighty

Bridging the gap between local businesses and the Generative AI era.

LocalMighty was founded by a Web Developer and SEO Specialist with a single mission: to ensure small businesses don't get left behind as search evolves from a list of links into AI-generated answers.

What We Do

We specialize in Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). We build tools and provide strategies that help your business stay visible in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

Our Expertise

  • Programmatic SEO: Building scalable tools that capture high-intent search traffic.
  • Technical Implementation: Leveraging modern stacks like Cloudflare Workers and Schema.org markup.
  • Content Strategy: Creating high-information-density content that AI models trust and cite.

Based on our experience building luxury travel platforms and micro-SaaS tools, we understand that "visibility" in 2026 requires more than just keywords—it requires a clear, machine-readable digital footprint.
